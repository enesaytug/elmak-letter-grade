The original histogram data provided. We convert this discrete data into a continuous probability distribution using Gaussian Kernel Density Estimation (KDE) as shown above.

The distribution was generated using Gaussian kernel density estimation (KDE) from the actual letter grade histogram of the 2025-2026 Spring term Electromechanical Energy Conversion course (65 students, 64 samples excluding VF). Each letter grade bucket was uniformly distributed into samples within its score range, then smoothed with a bandwidth of h = 5.

Letter grade thresholds are not based on the ITU absolute scale, but rather determined according to the histogram of the 2025-2026 Spring term Electromechanical Energy Conversion course.
